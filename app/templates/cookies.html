What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, enhance functionality, and improve your browsing experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as for authentication and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect data about how you use our site, allowing us to improve its performance and user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences, such as language or region settings, to provide a more personalized experience.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads to you and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

How We Use Cookies

  • To remember your preferences and settings.
  • To analyze site usage and improve user experience.
  • To deliver personalized content and ads.
  • To ensure website security and functionality.
